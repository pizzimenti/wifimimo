# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, technically constructive collaboration.

Participants should:

- focus on the code, behavior, and evidence
- assume good intent until facts show otherwise
- welcome good-faith questions and contributions
- disagree without insults, harassment, or personal attacks

The following are not acceptable:

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- abusive language or dogpiling
- sharing private information without permission
- repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or lock content that violates this code of conduct, and may restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness abusive behavior, contact the maintainer privately rather than escalating in a public thread.
